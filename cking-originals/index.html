C. KING is a clothing collection created by Baltimore born artists, Keith King and c.BARR(@n8tivalien) . With the use of found objects and hand-cut stencils, each garment is dyed and distressed to create a one of a kind work of expression. The graphics often promote ideas about knowledge and self actualization.
